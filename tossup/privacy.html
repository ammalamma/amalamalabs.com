Privacy Policy

Tossup - Random Decision Generator

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

Overview

Tossup ("the App") is developed by Amal Ama Labs ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use Tossup.

Information We Collect

Settings and Preferences

Tossup stores your custom settings and preferences, including:

  • Custom lists and options you create for decision tools
  • App preferences and configurations
  • Tool customizations

This data is stored locally on your device and synced to your private iCloud account if you have iCloud enabled for the App.

Subscription Information

If you subscribe to Tossup Pro, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. We receive only:

  • Confirmation of active subscription status
  • Subscription type (monthly or yearly)

Analytics (Future)

We may collect anonymous usage analytics in future versions to improve the App. This would include:

  • Which tools are used most frequently
  • General usage patterns
  • App performance data

Analytics data would be anonymous and not linked to your identity. We will update this policy before implementing analytics collection.

Information We Do Not Collect

Tossup does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Photos or media
  • Health or fitness data

No account is required to use Tossup.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to provide app functionality:

  • Save your custom settings and preferences
  • Sync your data across your Apple devices (if iCloud is enabled)
  • Verify subscription status for Pro features

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored:

  • On your device: All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • In iCloud (if enabled): Your data may sync to your private iCloud account, protected by your Apple ID

We do not operate external servers that store your personal data. Your information stays within Apple's ecosystem.

Third-Party Services

Tossup uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. They receive anonymous identifiers to track subscription status but no personal information. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple: If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in Apple's iCloud service under your account. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights and Choices

iCloud Sync

You can disable iCloud sync for Tossup in your device's Settings > Apple ID > iCloud.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Delete custom settings within the app
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling Tossup
  • Request complete data deletion by contacting us

Children's Privacy

Tossup is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from any users, including children. No account or personal information is required to use the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and, where appropriate, through the App or App Store.
