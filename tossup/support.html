Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tossup?

Tossup is a decision-making app with 12 random picker tools: Spin Wheel, Coin Flip, Dice Roller, Random Number, List Picker, Magic 8-Ball, Yes/No Oracle, Letter Generator, Team Generator, Finger Chooser, Lottery Picker, and Bingo Caller. All 12 tools are free to use.

What does Tossup Pro include?

Tossup Pro unlocks premium customization: color palettes, 3D dice themes, world coin designs, expanded limits, custom answer packs, and advanced settings. Available as a monthly subscription ($1.99/month), yearly subscription ($9.99/year), or a one-time lifetime purchase ($19.99).

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open Tossup, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase. If the restore doesn't work, try signing out and back in to the App Store, then tap Restore Purchases again.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Tossup Pro
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Your Pro features will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your custom content (wheel items, lists, settings) is stored locally on your device and never sent to any server. Tossup does not collect personal information, analytics, or advertising data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Does Tossup work on iPad?

Yes. Tossup is a universal app that works on both iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing or not responding

Try force-quitting and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store. You can also try uninstalling and reinstalling — your purchases can be restored via Settings > Restore Purchases.

My purchase isn't showing up

Go to Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchases. If you're still having trouble, make sure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID and check your internet connection.
