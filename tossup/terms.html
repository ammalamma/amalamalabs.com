Description of Service

Tossup is a random decision generator application that helps you make choices through various randomization tools, including:

  • Spin wheel
  • Random number generator
  • List randomizer
  • Yes/No oracle
  • Card draw
  • Color picker
  • Timer roulette

The App uses randomization algorithms to generate results. Results are random and for entertainment and decision-assistance purposes.
