# AmaLama Labs — Brand Spec

## What This Is

The foundational brand document for AmaLama Labs. Every piece of copy — website, Product Hunt, Reddit, App Store, pitch decks, press, social — derives from this spec. If it contradicts this document, it's off-brand.

---

## Brand Identity

**Name:** AmaLama Labs
**Type:** AI-native product lab
**Founded:** 2026
**Founder:** Amber Lee Coffman
**Tagline:** *Taste at scale.*

### What AmaLama Labs IS

An AI-native product lab that builds software people actually want to use. We built the engine — an intelligence system that evaluates markets, identifies opportunities, and ships products from concept to App Store. AI is the team. Taste is the filter. Every product that comes out of this lab has been pressure-tested, designed with intention, and built to a standard that most teams of fifty can't match.

### What AmaLama Labs IS NOT

- Not an indie developer shipping side projects
- Not an AI demo factory producing slop
- Not a services company
- Not a one-trick app shop

### The Core Tension (Our Positioning Moat)

**Everyone can build with AI now. Almost nobody builds with taste.**

AI democratized the ability to ship software. The market is flooded with AI-generated products that feel generated — generic, soulless, technically functional but emotionally empty. AmaLama Labs exists in the gap between what AI *can* build and what AI *should* build. The engine gives us speed and scale. The taste gives us products people love.

## Brand Story

### The Short Version (30 seconds)

AmaLama Labs is a product lab that built an AI-powered engine to find, evaluate, design, and ship software products. Not AI slop — products with real craft, built on first principles. Our flagship app, Toss Up, was entirely conceived, designed, and developed inside the engine. It's the first proof of what happens when you combine AI capability with product taste at a system level.

### The Full Version (2 minutes)

The software industry told everyone the same story: you need a team of fifty, millions in funding, and eighteen months to ship a product. That story is dead.

AmaLama Labs is what replaces it. We built an intelligence system — an engine that evaluates thousands of market opportunities, scores them across dozens of dimensions, and identifies the ones worth building. Then we build them. AI handles engineering, analysis, distribution optimization. What it doesn't handle — and what no AI handles well — is taste. Knowing what to build. Knowing when something feels right versus just looks right. Knowing what a user needs before they know it.

That's the lab. Engine + taste. Speed without slop.

Our flagship product, Toss Up, is proof of concept for the engine itself. Every decision — the category, the feature set, the design language, the monetization model, the App Store positioning — was run through the system. It wasn't a side project. It was a thesis test. And it shipped.

We're not building one app. We're building a product portfolio across high-value verticals — each one identified by the engine, refined by taste, and shipped at a pace that traditional teams can't match. The goal isn't to be a successful app developer. The goal is to prove that one operator with the right AI systems can outbuild and outship companies fifty times our size.

## Brand Pillars

### 1. Taste
The non-negotiable. Every product that leaves this lab has been designed with intention — not generated and shipped. Taste is what separates AmaLama Labs from every other AI builder. It's in the UX decisions, the copy, the features we cut, the problems we chose to solve. AI can build anything. We build the right things, the right way.

### 2. Engine
The system behind the lab. An intelligence pipeline that evaluates markets, scores opportunities, and accelerates every phase of product development. This isn't a person with ChatGPT. This is a purpose-built system — market intelligence, opportunity scoring, AI executive team, automated distribution — operating as a unified product machine.

### 3. Craft
Ambition without craft is noise. Every interaction, every screen, every word in our products is there on purpose. We don't ship MVPs and call them products. We ship products that feel finished on day one — because the engine lets us move fast enough to be thorough.

### 4. Customer Obsession
We build for the people who use our products. Not for investors. Not for press. Not for Product Hunt upvotes. The user is the point. Every product decision — what to build, what to cut, how to price it, how to talk about it — starts with: does this make the product better for the person using it?

## Brand Voice

### Personality

**Confident, not arrogant.** We know what we built and we're proud of it. We don't hedge or apologize. But we let the work speak — we don't need to tear anyone else down.

**Sharp, not clever.** Our copy is precise and purposeful. No filler, no corporate-speak, no buzzwords. We say what we mean in as few words as possible. If a sentence doesn't earn its place, it gets cut.

**Warm, not cute.** We're human and approachable. We care about our users and it shows. But we're not quirky-startup-voice. No "oopsie!" error messages. No "we're just getting started on this wild journey!" We're a lab, not a lemonade stand.

**Ambitious, not delusional.** We're building something big and we say so. But we back it up with shipped products, not pitch decks. Every claim has proof behind it.

### Voice Spectrum

| Dimension | Position |
|-----------|----------|
| Formal ←→ Direct | 75% direct |
| Reserved ←→ Confident | 80% confident |
| Technical ←→ Accessible | 70% accessible |
| Corporate ←→ Human | 85% human |
| Humble ←→ Bold | 70% bold |

### Words We Use
- Ship, build, craft, engine, lab, taste, precision, intentional, obsessed, prove, real
- "We built..." "We ship..." "We don't..."

### Words We Don't Use
- Synergy, leverage, disrupt, pivot, hack, guru, ninja, passionate
- "We're excited to announce..." "We're humbled..." "Stay tuned..."
- Anything that sounds like a press release or a LinkedIn post

## The Lab's "Features" (What Makes This a Lab, Not a Dev)

When talking about AmaLama Labs as an entity, these are the capabilities:

1. **Market Intelligence Engine** — Processes signals from 87+ sources. Evaluates thousands of opportunities. Scores on competition, revenue potential, time to market, distribution automation, AI buildability.

2. **AI Executive Team** — 23 domain-expert AI consultants with persistent memory across sessions. Strategy, product, growth, brand, legal, finance — the full C-suite, AI-native.

3. **Product Development Pipeline** — From opportunity identification to App Store submission. AI handles engineering. Human taste handles everything else.

4. **Distribution Automation** — ASO optimization, keyword research, competitive monitoring, cross-product growth. Built to scale without scaling headcount.

5. **First-Principles Methodology** — Every product decision is grounded in market data, competitive analysis, and user need — not guesses. Best practices aren't aspirational. They're the floor.

## Visual Identity Notes (for Creative Director)

The current site aesthetic (dark, indigo-violet gradient, grain texture, glassmorphism) should evolve to match the brand:

- **Dark = authority.** Keep it.
- **Grain texture = craft.** Signals handmade, intentional. Keep it.
- **Typography should feel precise, not decorative.** Inter + JetBrains Mono is the right pairing — clean, technical, modern.
- **The visual story is: serious lab, beautiful output.** Think less "startup landing page," more "design studio with engineering rigor."

## Usage Rules

1. **Always "AmaLama Labs"** — not "Amalama," not "AML," not "the lab" in external copy (internal shorthand is fine).
2. **Products are "by AmaLama Labs"** — endorsed, not branded-house.
3. **Never say "solo developer" or "indie dev"** — we are a product lab.
4. **Never apologize for team size.** We don't mention it unless it serves the narrative (and when it does, it's a flex, not a disclaimer).
5. **AI is a capability, not a caveat.** We don't say "built with AI" defensively. We say it confidently — and immediately follow with proof of quality.
6. **Taste is always in the conversation.** If we mention AI, taste is the counterweight. They're a pair. Engine and taste. Speed and craft. Always.
